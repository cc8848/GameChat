Invitations

How to Send Invitations

  • First select what groups and rooms you want to include in your invitation (unless you are already in a group context, in which case GameChat defaults the group)
  • Can't send invitations to protected users (invite their chaperone account, instead)
  • Once the invitation is accepted, the invited user can join all public rooms in the target group(s)

Send mail to support@pajato.com.
