Get Started

You can use GameChat to chat and play games with friends and family. When playing a game, you can use chat features to communicate with other uses and mentor and teach someone else the game. For example, you can teach a child or a grandchild to play chess.

GameChat is automatically updated across multiple devices, so you can start a game or conversation on one device, such as a tablet, and continue it on another, such as your smartphone.

What you can do with GameChat

  • Send messages: You can start a conversation with another GameChat user in either a private conversation or in a group chat.
  • Play games: You can play a game either locally, with a friend who is sharing a device with you, against the computer, or with another GameChat member who is using their own device, possibly located somewhere else.

What you need to use GameChat

  • An Android device: You can play GameChat on any Android device running TBD or later.
  • A valid email, Google or Facebook account: You can log into GameChat using either a email address or your existing Google or Facebook account.
